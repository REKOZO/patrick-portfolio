Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take to my country?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method you choose. Typically:

  • Europe: 5-10 business days after we ship your package
  • North America: 7-14 business days
  • Asia/Australia: 10-21 business days

These estimates don't include the 1-3 business days we need to process and prepare your order for shipment.

What fees should I expect when using FranceBuyProxy?

Our fee structure is transparent:

  • Service fee: 10% of item price (minimum €5 per item)
  • Shipping fee: Actual carrier cost + 15% handling fee
  • Optional services: Gift wrapping (€5), priority processing (€10)

You'll see a complete breakdown of all fees before confirming any purchase.

Can I return items purchased through FranceBuyProxy?

Returns are possible but subject to the original store's return policy and the following conditions:

  • You must notify us within 14 days of receiving your item
  • The item must be in original condition with all tags attached
  • You're responsible for return shipping costs to our France warehouse
  • A €15 restocking fee applies for processed returns

We'll help facilitate the return process with the original store once we receive the item back.

How does package consolidation work?

Our package consolidation service can save you up to 60% on shipping costs:

  1. We receive your purchases at our French warehouse
  2. We repackage items together (removing excess packaging when possible)
  3. We calculate the most cost-effective shipping method based on total weight/dimensions
  4. You pay only one international shipping fee for the consolidated package

You can request consolidation in your account settings or during checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfers (for orders over €300)
  • FranceBuyProxy Wallet (preload funds for faster checkout)

All transactions are securely processed with 3D Secure authentication.
