How Store Requests Work

Can't find your favorite French store in our supported list? Request it here and we'll work to add support as quickly as possible.

Our Process:

  • We review each store request within 2 business days
  • We assess technical feasibility and purchasing requirements
  • You'll receive email updates about your request status
  • Most stores are added within 1-2 weeks of request

What We Look For:

  • Stores must ship within France (no international shipping)
  • Stores must have a secure checkout process
  • Product availability must be reliably displayed
  • Clear return policies are preferred
