Calculator FAQs

Our calculator provides highly accurate estimates based on current exchange rates and our extensive shipping data. The product prices are verified directly from the French stores when you add items. The only potential variance would be:

  • Final customs duties (though we estimate these based on your country's regulations)
  • If product prices change between calculation and purchase
  • If you modify package dimensions/weight from the estimate

Yes! Our calculator supports adding unlimited items from any French stores. When you add items from multiple stores, we'll automatically calculate the most cost-effective way to consolidate them into fewer packages to save you money on international shipping.

We calculate estimated import duties based on:

  • Your destination country's customs regulations
  • The declared value of your items
  • The type of products (different duty rates apply to different categories)
  • Any applicable trade agreements between France and your country

For most countries, we're able to estimate duties within ±15% accuracy. Some countries like Canada and Australia have very predictable duty calculations, while others like Brazil and India can be more variable.
