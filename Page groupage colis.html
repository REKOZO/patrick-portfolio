Groupage Shipping FAQs

How much can I save with groupage shipping?

Savings vary based on the number of packages and destination, but most customers save 30-60% compared to shipping items individually. Our calculator above can give you a personalized estimate.

How long will my items stay in your warehouse?

We provide free storage for up to 45 days. After that, a small daily storage fee applies. You can configure automatic shipping thresholds in your account settings.

Can I combine purchases from different French stores?

Absolutely! That's the main benefit of our service. We'll receive packages from any French store and consolidate them into one shipment to your country.

How does customs declaration work for consolidated packages?

We prepare a complete customs declaration listing all items in your consolidated package with their correct values. You can choose between automatic handling or providing your own customs information.

What if some items are delayed from the stores?

You can set preferences in your account - either ship complete orders only, or ship available items first with partial shipments. We'll notify you of any delays.
