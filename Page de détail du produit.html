Detailed Description

Dior Sauvage is a fresh, spicy fragrance for the self-assured man. Created by perfumer François Demachy, Sauvage is a nod to the wide-open spaces under a blue sky that stretches as far as the eye can see.

Top Notes:

Calabrian bergamot - A burst of freshness from this Italian citrus fruit

Middle Notes:

Sichuan pepper - A spicy, vibrant note that electrifies the composition

Base Notes:

Ambroxan - A modern amber note that leaves a memorable trail

How to Use:

Spray on pulse points (wrists, neck) for optimal diffusion. Avoid contact with clothes.
