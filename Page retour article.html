Return Policy Details

FranceBuyProxy facilitates returns between international customers and French retailers. Our policy is designed to comply with French consumer law while accounting for the complexities of international proxy purchases.

Important Return Conditions
  • Items must be returned in original condition with all tags attached
  • Original packaging should be included when possible
  • Return requests must be initiated within 14 days of delivery
  • Items must arrive at our French warehouse within 21 days of return approval

Eligible for Return:

  • Faulty or damaged items (must report within 48 hours of delivery)
  • Items that don't match their description
  • Unwanted items (must be unused and in original condition)

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Perishable goods (food, flowers, etc.)
  • Intimate apparel, swimwear, and cosmetics for hygiene reasons
  • Items marked as "non-returnable" by the original retailer

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have to return an item?

You must initiate your return request within 14 days of receiving your order. The physical return must arrive at our French warehouse within 21 days of return approval.

Who pays for return shipping?

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error or a defective product. We can arrange return shipping at competitive rates.

What if the original store refuses my return?

While rare, if a store refuses a return that meets their policy, we'll work with them to resolve the issue. If unsuccessful, we may offer store credit as a goodwill gesture.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Exchanges are handled as a return and new purchase. You'll need to return the original item and place a new order for the replacement.
