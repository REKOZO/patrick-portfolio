Frequently Asked Questions

You can shop from virtually any French online store! We have special integrations with major retailers like Sephora FR, FNAC, Galeries Lafayette, and more, but you can also paste product links from any French website. Our team will verify if we can purchase from that store.

The total time depends on several factors:

  • 1 1-2 business days to purchase the item from the French store
  • 2 2-4 business days for the item to reach our warehouse
  • 3 1 business day for processing at our warehouse
  • 4 5-14 business days for international shipping (depending on option chosen)

Total estimated time: 2-3 weeks for standard shipping.

We handle all customs documentation and declarations for you. Any applicable import taxes or duties will be calculated during checkout so there are no surprises when your package arrives. The amount depends on your country's regulations and the value/type of goods.

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for most items. However, return shipping to France is the customer's responsibility, and original shipping fees are non-refundable. Some items (like personalized products or cosmetics) may not be returnable due to French store policies.

When you purchase multiple items from different French stores, we'll hold them at our warehouse and combine them into a single international shipment. This can significantly reduce your overall shipping costs. You can request consolidation in your account settings or during checkout.
